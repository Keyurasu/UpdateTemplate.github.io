About Me

Assistant Teaching Professor with 20+ years of experience in Information Systems

As an Assistant Teaching Professor at Arizona State University's W. P. Carey School of Business, I bring over two decades of expertise in information systems and instructional design to deliver innovative education through some of the largest format courses in the school.

My approach combines research, creativity, and technical expertise to deliver solutions that exceed expectations. I specialize in developing student-centered curricula that foster critical thinking, hands-on skills, and inclusivity, consistently achieving evaluation scores of 6.3–6.7/7.

I am committed to advancing ASU's mission through curriculum innovation, mentorship, and departmental expertise, preparing students for real-world business challenges while serving as the Information Systems faculty representative to the University Senate.
